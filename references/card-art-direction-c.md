# Full Deck Art Direction C

## Style anchor

Modern meticulous Chinese gongbi fantasy with fine line drawing, clean editorial color blocks, silk texture, refined faces, airy compositions, and strong mobile readability.

Use a high-dopamine palette across the deck: violet, cyan blue, jade green, tangerine, warm yellow, rose pink, cinnabar, and restrained gold. Keep ink-black areas for contrast.

Generate full-bleed artwork without a frame. The HTML supplies the shared border. This reduces pseudo-text and keeps all 78 cards visually consistent.

## Suit language

| Suit | Eastern object language | Dominant palette |
|---|---|---|
| Wands | flowering wooden staffs, fire, birds, banners | tangerine, cinnabar, violet |
| Cups | porcelain cups, lotus water, rain, reflections | cyan, cobalt, rose pink |
| Swords | straight jian swords, wind, clouds, winter light | cobalt, violet, acid green |
| Pentacles | jade bi discs, gold seals, gardens, grain | jade green, warm yellow, coral |

## Shared constraints

- Original composition and culturally coherent Chinese visual language.
- No text, calligraphy, pseudo-letters, numbers, labels, title plaques, logos, signatures, watermarks, talismans, or religious icons.
- No generated frame, border, caption space, signboard, scroll, or inscribed pillar.
- No Western medieval clothing, European occult symbols, direct RWS copy, photorealism, or 3D render.
- Vertical 2:3, one clear focal hierarchy, readable at mobile card size.

## Output

- Source generation: `768x1152` JPEG under ignored `assets/images/generated-sources/full-deck-c/`.
- Web final: normalized JPEG under `assets/images/cards/` using the canonical filenames from `references/deck.json`.
